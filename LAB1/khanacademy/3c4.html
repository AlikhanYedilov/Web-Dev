Recipe #1 - Doner

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken or beef500 g
Yogurt200 g
Garlic2 cloves
Pita bread4
VegetablesAs desired

Step 1: Marinate the meat with yogurt, garlic, and spices for 1 hour.

Step 2: Grill or fry the meat until fully cooked.

Step 3: Wrap the meat and vegetables in pita bread.

Recipe #2 - Sushi

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Sushi rice2 cups
Rice vinegar50 ml
Fish (salmon/tuna)200 g
Nori sheets4
VegetablesAs desired

Step 1: Cook and season the rice with vinegar.

Step 2: Place nori on a rolling mat and spread rice over it.

Step 3: Add fish and vegetables, then roll tightly.

Step 4: Cut into pieces and serve.
